Step 1

$row(i+1) - row(i) [i=1,2,3,4,5,…,N-1] $

Step 2

$row(i+1) - 2^2row(i) [i=2,3,4,5,…,N-1] $

Step 3

$row(i+1) - 3^2row(i) [i=3,4,5,…,N-1] $

Step k

$row(i+1) - k^2row(i) [i=k,k+1,…,N-1] $
…

Step N-1
